About

Survii is a multiple-choice survey app that provides a platform for people to manage (as Creators) and answer (as Respondents) online surveys.

How it works

Survey Creators can view, create, update, and delete their surveys. They can keep track of the number of responses, view the results, and close their surveys, among other features. Survey results are private, and only the survey creator can see them.

Survey Respondents can view and answer surveys that the Survey Creators publish. They can submit survey responses anonymously as they will only get added to the list of respondents without revealing their responses.
